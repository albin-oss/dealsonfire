# Interview Scripts

Non-leading, short, verbatim-recorded. Never explain the product's intent
before a question; never rescue a silence — silences are answers.

## Merchant — same day as first-hour observation (~15 min)

Warm-up
1. "Walk me through what you just made, as if I hadn't been here."

The experience
2. "What surprised you?"
3. "What felt slower than you expected?"
4. "When did you hesitate?"
5. "What almost stopped you?"
6. "Was there a word on screen you'd never use yourself?" *(hunting jargon leaks)*

Value
7. "When did DOF feel useful?"
8. "When did it feel unnecessary?"
9. "How would you describe DOF to another maker, in one sentence?" *(their
   positioning ≠ ours; record verbatim)*

Forward
10. "What do you expect to happen in the next week without you doing anything?"
11. "What will you do on DOF tomorrow, honestly?"

## Merchant — day 10–12 (~10 min)

1. "Show me the last thing you published. Why that?"
2. "What did you stop doing after the first day?"
3. "Did anyone say anything to you about your shop? What exactly?"
4. "What did you check when you opened DOF — and what did you hope it would say?"
5. "If DOF disappeared tomorrow, what would you actually miss?"

## Customer — after arriving on a shared link (~5 min)

1. "What is this page, in your own words?"
2. "What did you tap first? Why?"
3. "What does Follow do, do you think?" *(mental model, before explaining)*
4. "Would you come back without being sent a link? What for?"
5. "What almost made you close the tab?"

## Recording rules

- Verbatim quotes only; interpretation happens later, in the friction log.
- Note question number + timestamp so quotes trace back to context.
- Every answer that names a struggle becomes a FRICTION_LOG.md entry the same
  day, cross-referenced to the interview.
